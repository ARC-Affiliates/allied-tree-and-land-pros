#Myths vs. Facts

Myth: Topping a tree makes it safer in storms.

Fact: Topping creates weak regrowth and increases failure risk; ANSI A300 prohibits it as a standard practice.

Myth: Any local provider can prune commercial trees.

Fact: Commercial pruning requires certified arborist judgment, OSHA-compliant rigging, and $1M+ liability coverage.

Myth: Stumps will rot away on their own quickly.

Fact: Hardwood stumps in WV clay can persist 7–10 years and attract termites and carpenter ants near buildings.

Myth: Tree work is cheaper in summer.

Fact: Late winter (February–March) is typically 10–20% cheaper because crews have lower demand.
