#Myths vs Facts About Affordable Tree Service

Myth: The cheapest quote is the best deal.

Fact: Quotes 50%+ below market usually exclude insurance, haul-away, or stump work — final cost climbs higher than the median quote.

Myth: All tree services have insurance.

Fact: Roughly 1 in 3 cash operators in rural WV carry no liability coverage. Always request the Certificate of Insurance.

Myth: Topping a tree saves money long-term.

Fact: Topping causes regrowth that requires re-pruning every 2–3 years, tripling 10-year cost (source: isa-arbor.com).

Myth: Stump grinding is always included.

Fact: Stump grinding is a separate $90–$400 line item in 2026 Augusta, WV pricing.
